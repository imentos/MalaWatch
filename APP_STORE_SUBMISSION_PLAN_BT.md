### Description

```text
A quiet mala bead counter for daily prayer, mantra and meditation practice.

MalaWatch combines a tactile 3D bead wheel, Om Mani Padme Hum syllable guidance, voice support and haptic feedback, giving you a simple way to keep count on iPhone and Apple Watch without turning practice into a productivity task.

Feel each bead
The 3D beads move with your gesture. Swipe down to count one bead, with a gentle haptic response for each count. Choose 27, 54 or 108 beads as your round goal, and receive a clear completion response when a round is finished.

Follow the six-syllable mantra
The guide shows Om, Ma, Ni, Pad, Me, Hum one syllable at a time. Follow mode speaks each syllable aloud; Silent mode keeps the guide visual only for quiet spaces.

Track your rounds
Your current bead count and completed rounds are shown clearly. Progress is saved on your device, so you can leave the app and continue later.

Count from your wrist
The Apple Watch app supports tap and Digital Crown counting, with haptic feedback for each bead. It is useful while walking, commuting, meditating or practising without holding your phone.

Choose your bead style
Seven bead styles are included: Basic Wood, Rosewood, Walnut, Agarwood, Jade, Obsidian and Graphite. All current styles are included without additional purchase.

Offline and private
No registration, no login and no network connection are required. Your practice data stays on your own device.

For many traditions and daily practices
MalaWatch can support Buddhist mantra practice, prayer beads, japa, rosary-style counting, breathwork, affirmations or any personal daily practice where you want the count to stay in the background.

Terms of Use: https://imentos.github.io/MalaWatch/terms.html
Privacy Policy: https://imentos.github.io/MalaWatch/privacy-policy.html
```

## App Review Notes

Use the same English App Review Notes as the main submission:

```text
MalaWatch does not require an account or login.

This build includes an optional consumable support purchase. It does not unlock content, subscriptions, Apple Watch access, bead styles, or any practice feature. All current app features, including Apple Watch support and bead styles, are available without purchase.

Support product: rkuo.MalaWatch.support.small, type: Consumable. This is a voluntary tip/support purchase only and has no restorable entitlement.

The app is an offline mala / prayer bead counter for iPhone and Apple Watch. Core flows: launch the app, swipe down on the 3D bead wheel to count, follow the Om Mani Padme Hum syllable guide, change the bead goal between 27/54/108, choose a bead style, switch voice mode between Follow and Silent, and reset the current round.

MalaWatch does not collect user data, does not use third-party SDKs, and does not require network access for core functionality. Voice guidance uses Apple's on-device AVSpeechSynthesizer.
```
