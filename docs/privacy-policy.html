Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

MalaWatch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your practice stays entirely on your device.

This Privacy Policy describes how MalaWatch ("we", "the app") handles information when you use the MalaWatch iOS and Apple Watch application.

Data We Do Not Collect

MalaWatch collects no personal information whatsoever. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash telemetry
  • Health or fitness data
  • Any data that could identify you as an individual

There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no user profiles.

How Your Data Is Stored

Local Storage Only

All app data — your bead count, round progress, selected bead theme, and bead goal — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

No Internet Connection Required

MalaWatch works entirely offline. The app never makes network requests. No data is sent to any server at any time.

Third-Party Services

Voice Synthesis (On-Device)

MalaWatch uses Apple's AVSpeechSynthesizer framework to speak chant syllables aloud in Follow mode. This technology runs entirely on your device — no audio is recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

Optional Support Purchase

MalaWatch offers an optional consumable support purchase using Apple In-App Purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple. The app does not collect payment information, and support purchases do not unlock additional app features.

No Third-Party SDKs

MalaWatch contains no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or any other external frameworks. The app uses only Apple's first-party frameworks.

Children's Privacy

MalaWatch is rated 4+ and is appropriate for all ages. Because we collect no personal data from anyone, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date. As a fully offline app with no data collection, significant changes are unlikely.
